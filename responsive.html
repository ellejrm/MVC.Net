The Main

Chania (also spelled Hania) is the capital city, a place where different civilizations have flourished throughout the centuries. Strolling around the Old Town's maze-like alleys with the beautiful Venetian mansions, the fountains and the churches will guide you through well-preserved historical monuments.

What?

Chania also spelled Hania, is a city in Greece and the capital of the Chania regional unit.

Where?

Chania is one of the most famous islands in Greece with great historical and natural interest.

How?

Chania is built on the site of ancient Kydonia, a city that according to legend was founded in prehistoric times by Kydonas, a son of Hermes or maybe the god Apollo.
